About Me

A working IT student is someone who balances their academic studies in Information Technology with part-time or full-time employment. They often manage a busy schedule, juggling coursework, projects, and job responsibilities. These students gain practical experience in the field while enhancing their skills through real-world applications, which can include roles such as tech support, software development, or IT internships. This hands-on experience helps them build their career while still completing their degree. Managing time effectively is key to succeeding as a working IT student.
